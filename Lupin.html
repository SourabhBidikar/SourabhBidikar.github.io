Sales Report with Power BI
for Lupin Meds

Cleaned and extracted necesaary data, worked on data modelling,
created useful measures and calculated columns with DAX and developed
a comprehensive, interactive and attractive 3 paged power BI report with
drill through capabilities.

Dataset:   Lupin Meds Dataset   contains tables like:

Fact table: Sales Details and German Sales details (extracted by me).
Dimension tables: Customer Details, Product Details, Region, DimDate (created by me).

Apart from the available data I created different measures to extract needed data from the dataset like:
1.   Total Sales = SUMX('Sales Details','Sales Details'[Quantity]*'Sales Details'[unit price])
2.   Total Cost = SUMX('Sales Details','Sales Details'[Quantity]*'Sales Details'[unit cost])
3.  Profit margin= DIVIDE([Total Profit],[Total Sales])

Year wise Sales using:
1.  Sales 2019 = CALCULATE([Total Sales],FILTER(Date,Date[Year]=2019))
2.  Sales 2020 = CALCULATE([Total Sales],FILTER(Date,Date[Year]=2020))

For Top 10 products using DAX:
Top 10 Products= CALCULATE([Total Sales), FILTER(Values('product details'[product name]),
IF(RANKX(ALL('product details'[product name],[Total Sales], , Desc)<=10,[Total Sales], Blank())).
